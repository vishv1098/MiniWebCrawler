About iPhone-5s

 The pocket-friendly iPhone 5s, which uses Apple's newest operating system (iOS 7), is hard to beat when it comes to intuitive, foolproof access to core smart-phone functions. Its camera takes some of the best stills and videos you can get with a smart phone. Its nifty finger print reader, built into the Home button, lets you safely and quickly unlock the phone's screen or authorize an iTunes purchase with a light press of your finger. Just don't expect the giant screen or some of the cutting-edge features being offered by some competing models.
