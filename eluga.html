About Panasonic Eluga U

 Panasonic has launched its Eluga U smartphone (already available in the global market) for the Indian market today. The smartphone with KitKat 4.4.2 Operating System (OS) on board comes with Snapdragon 400 processor. The new Eluga U comes powered by a 2 GB RAM and 16 GB internal memory, that can be expanded up to 32 GB. The back cover has a premium mirror printed texture that echoes a Galaxy S5 like texture (dotted) style.

 The back side has got an anti-shatter crystal glass for classy looks and durability. At the launch event Manish Sharma, Managing Director Panasonic Indian said, the compnay aims to launch 15 new phones and 8 new feature phones in the Indian market and will look at grabbing 5% share of the Indian market in coming a years.
